To Calculate Total Hours, type in the hour of each week and the hourly wage and the respective fields, then click Calculate Totals.

To store totals in the server, click Store totals in log (can only be done after calculating totals)

To clear the log in the server, click on Clear Log.

To update the Log and Grand Totals to show on the page, click on Update Log and Grand Totals.

You can add multiple entries to the log after calculating other totals.
